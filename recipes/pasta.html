Pasta Penne alla Bolognese

Description

Penne alla Bolognese is a comforting Italian pasta dish made with ground meat simmered in a rich tomato sauce, seasoned with herbs, and served with grated cheese. It's ideal for a satisfying lunch or dinner.

Ingredients

  • 400 g (about 14 oz) penne pasta
  • 300 g (10 oz) ground beef or beef/pork mix
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 garlic clove, minced1 garlic clove, minced
  • 400 ml (1 can) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano or Italian herbs
  • Freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • Optional: a splash of red wine or a pinch of sugar

Steps

  1. Cook the penne pasta in salted boiling water according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pan, heat olive oil and sauté the onion and garlic until soft.
  3. Add the ground meat and cook until browned.
  4. Pour in the crushed tomatoes and season with salt, pepper, and herbs. Let simmer for 15–20 minutes. Add wine or sugar if desired.
  5. Combine the cooked pasta with the sauce and mix well.
  6. Serve hot, topped with grated Parmesan and freshly ground black pepper.
