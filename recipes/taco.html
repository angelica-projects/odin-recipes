Taco

Description

Tacos are one of the most iconic dishes in Mexican cuisine. This basic and traditional recipe uses soft corn tortillas filled with seasoned meat, topped with onion, cilantro, and salsa to taste. They're easy to prepare, delicious, and perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 500 g (about 1 lb) of meat (beef, chicken, or pork, as preferred)
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1/2 onion, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon cumin or taco seasoning (optional)
  • 8–10 corn tortillas
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Lime wedges
  • Salsa (green, red, or your preferred kind)

Steps

  1. In a hot pan with oil, sauté the garlic and onion until golden.
  2. Add the finely chopped or sliced meat. Cook until it's well browned and fully cooked.
  3. Season with salt, pepper, and cumin or taco seasoning to taste.
  4. Warm the corn tortillas on a griddle or skillet for a few seconds on each side.
  5. Fill each tortilla with the cooked meat.
  6. Top with chopped onion, cilantro, and a squeeze of lime juice.
  7. Serve with your favorite salsa.
